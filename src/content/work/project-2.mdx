## Overview

A complete website redesign for a technology company looking to modernize their online presence and better communicate their services to potential clients.

## Key Features

- **Content Management**: Easy-to-use CMS for non-technical staff to update content
- **Service Pages**: Dedicated pages for each service offering with detailed information
- **Team Section**: Showcase of team members and company culture
- **Contact Forms**: Multiple contact forms with validation and spam protection

## Technologies Used

- WordPress
- Custom Theme Development
- Advanced Custom Fields
- Contact Form 7
- Custom JavaScript

## Results

The new website improved lead generation by 45% and reduced bounce rate by 30%. The content management system allows the team to update the site without developer assistance.
